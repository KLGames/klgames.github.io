1. INTRODUCTION
----------------------------------------

This is a climbing on obi rope system, where with your humanoid character, you can climb the obi rope in 2D/2.5D OR 3D. Look at the Demo scene under the folder KLGames/ClimbObiRope. YOU HAVE TO INSTALL FIRST THE OBI ROPE PACKAGE FROM THE ASSSET-STORE! THIS IS ONLY AN EXTENSION!

The demo not contains any sound files!

OBI ROPE CLIMBING SYSTEM FEATURES

-2.5D/3D dimensions
-change rope/chain max velocity
-rope and chain
-climb up/down
-turn around on rope
-rope/chain sounds on move
-wall handle in front of player
-IK handling (arms, legs, head, body)
-swinging on rope front/back/left/right
-auto fall down from rope after X seconds (player tired)

----------------------------------------
2. RopesManager
----------------------------------------

In the demo scene I wrote fast a little character controller script, but if you have an other character controller, you can just quickly see the script and you can update the codebase. The codebase is structured and written in Rider.
RopesManager2D/3D supports IK handling (swinging on rope) on your character, or if you want just turn it off.
The RopesManager supports chains and ropes.


PlayerAndObiRopeCollision2D/3D is a class where the contact between the player and the rope will be.
CreateClimbableObiRope2D/3D is the main mechanic, where the rope generated and the climbing behaviours.
CreateNotClimbableObiRope3D is an obi rope/chain generator with gizmos, but it is not climbable.

----------------------------------------
3. CONTROLS
----------------------------------------

You can control the character with left/right and space. Space for jumping on the ground, or jumping down from the rope. Fast double left or right key just changes the character facing on the rope. The character jump down when tired or reach the bottom.

4. HDRP/ETC Upgrade
